# Webinar: Apply for Small Business Certification

**Phase:** Phase 4 - Business Enhancement (Once)  
**Duration:** 60 minutes  
**Level:** Intermediate  
**Prerequisites:** Established federal contracting presence

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this webinar, participants will be able to:
- Understand available small business certifications
- Determine eligibility for certifications
- Navigate certification application processes
- Understand benefits of each certification
- Prepare certification applications

## Agenda

### Introduction (5 minutes)
- Welcome and Phase 4 context
- Why certifications matter
- Certification options overview

### Certification Types (20 minutes)
- **HUBZone Program**
  - Eligibility requirements
  - Benefits
  - Application process
  - Maintenance
  
- **WOSB/EDWOSB Program**
  - Women-Owned Small Business
  - Economically Disadvantaged WOSB
  - Eligibility
  - Application
  
- **VOSB/SDVOSB Programs**
  - Veteran-Owned Small Business
  - Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business
  - VA verification
  - Benefits
  
- **Self-Certification vs. Third-Party**
  - When self-certification is allowed
  - Third-party certification requirements
  - SBA certification portal

### Application Process (20 minutes)
- **Pre-Application Preparation**
  - Eligibility assessment
  - Documentation gathering
  - Required information
  
- **Application Steps**
  - Portal access
  - Information entry
  - Document submission
  - Review and submit
  
- **Timeline and Review**
  - Processing time
  - Requests for information
  - Approval process
  - Maintaining certification

### Benefits and Strategy (10 minutes)
- **Certification Benefits**
  - Set-aside opportunities
  - Sole-source opportunities
  - Competitive advantages
  
- **Multiple Certifications**
  - Strategic selection
  - Maximizing opportunities
  - Maintenance considerations

### Wrap-up & Next Steps (5 minutes)
- Key takeaways
- Action items
- Next webinar: 8(a) Certification

## Key Takeaways
1. Multiple certification options exist with different benefits
2. Eligibility must be verified before applying
3. Documentation is critical for successful applications
4. Certifications open access to set-aside opportunities
5. Strategic certification selection maximizes opportunities
