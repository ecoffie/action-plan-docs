# Webinar: How to Find Bid Opportunities

**Phase:** Phase 2 - Bidding (Repeat)  
**Duration:** 60 minutes  
**Level:** Beginner/Intermediate  
**Prerequisites:** Phase 1 complete, SAM.gov registered

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this webinar, participants will be able to:
- Use multiple sources to find federal contracting opportunities
- Search effectively on SAM.gov and other platforms
- Set up opportunity alerts and notifications
- Understand different opportunity types (RFP, RFQ, RFI, Sources Sought)
- Evaluate opportunities using bid/no-bid criteria

**Filters:**
- **NAICS Codes**: Filter by your relevant NAICS codes
- **Agency**: Filter by specific agencies
- **Location**: Filter by geographic location
- **Set-Aside**: Filter by small business set-asides (8(a), HUBZone, WOSB, SDVOSB, etc.)
- **Solicitation Type**: RFP, RFQ, RFI, Sources Sought, etc.
- **Active Date Range**: When opportunities were posted
- **Response Deadline**: Filter by response deadlines

- Complex requirements

- Comprehensive proposals

- Management approach
- Pricing
- Longer response times

**RFQ (Request for Quote):**
- Simpler requirements
