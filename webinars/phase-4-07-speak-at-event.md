# Webinar: Speak at an Event

**Phase:** Phase 4 - Business Enhancement (Once)  
**Duration:** 30-45 minutes  
**Level:** Intermediate  
**Prerequisites:** Some expertise in federal contracting

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this webinar, participants will be able to:
- Understand benefits of speaking at events
- Identify speaking opportunities
- Develop speaking topics
- Create effective speaking proposals
- Deliver impactful presentations

## Agenda

### Introduction (5 minutes)
- Welcome and context
- Why speak at events?
- Benefits overview

### Benefits of Speaking (10 minutes)
- **Thought Leadership**
  - Industry recognition
  - Credibility building
  - Visibility
  - Expertise demonstration
  
- **Business Development**
  - Networking opportunities
  - Lead generation
  - Relationship building
  - Opportunity identification

### Finding Speaking Opportunities (10 minutes)
- **Types of Events**
  - Industry conferences
  - Trade shows
  - Association meetings
  - Webinars
  - Workshops
  
- **Where to Find Opportunities**
  - Conference websites
  - Industry associations
  - Event organizers
  - Call for speakers

### Developing Topics (8 minutes)
- **Topic Selection**
  - Your expertise areas
  - Audience needs
  - Current trends
  - Value proposition
  
- **Presentation Ideas**
  - Best practices
  - Case studies
  - Lessons learned
  - How-to sessions

### Speaking Proposals (8 minutes)
- **Proposal Elements**
  - Title
  - Abstract
  - Learning objectives
  - Speaker bio
  - Value proposition
  
- **Submitting Proposals**
  - Following guidelines
  - Professional presentation
  - Timely submission

### Delivery Tips (2 minutes)
- **Presentation Best Practices**
  - Preparation
  - Engagement
  - Professional delivery
  - Follow-up

## Key Takeaways
1. Speaking builds thought leadership and credibility
2. Multiple types of speaking opportunities exist
3. Topic selection should match expertise and audience needs
4. Professional proposals increase acceptance chances
5. Speaking provides business development opportunities
