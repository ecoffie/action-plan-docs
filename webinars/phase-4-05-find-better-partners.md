# Webinar: Find Better Partners

**Phase:** Phase 4 - Business Enhancement (Once)  
**Duration:** 45 minutes  
**Level:** Intermediate  
**Prerequisites:** Experience with teaming

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this webinar, participants will be able to:
- Evaluate current partner relationships
- Identify ideal partner characteristics
- Find strategic partners
- Build stronger partnerships
- Manage partner relationships effectively

## Agenda

### Introduction (5 minutes)
- Welcome and context
- Why better partners matter
- Strategic partnerships

### Evaluating Current Partners (12 minutes)
- **Partner Assessment**
  - Performance evaluation
  - Value contribution
  - Relationship quality
  - Strategic fit
  
- **Identifying Gaps**
  - Capability gaps
  - Performance issues
  - Relationship challenges
  - Strategic misalignment

### Ideal Partner Characteristics (12 minutes)
- **Strategic Fit**
  - Complementary capabilities
  - Geographic coverage
  - Size and capacity
  - Cultural alignment
  
- **Performance Factors**
  - Past performance quality
  - Financial stability
  - Reliability
  - Commitment

### Finding Better Partners (12 minutes)
- **Where to Look**
  - Industry associations
  - Networking events
  - Referrals
  - Competitive analysis
  
- **Evaluation Process**
  - Due diligence
  - Reference checks
  - Capability assessment
  - Strategic fit analysis

### Building Strong Partnerships (4 minutes)
- **Best Practices**
  - Clear agreements
  - Communication
  - Mutual benefit
  - Long-term focus

## Key Takeaways
1. Strategic partners enhance competitive position
2. Evaluate partners regularly
3. Seek complementary capabilities
4. Build relationships for long-term success
5. Quality partnerships are strategic assets
