# Webinar: Subcontractor Compliance

**Phase:** Phase 5 - Contract Management  
**Duration:** 60 minutes  
**Level:** Intermediate  
**Prerequisites:** Contract with subcontractors

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this webinar, participants will be able to:
- Understand subcontractor compliance requirements
- Manage subcontractor relationships
- Ensure compliance with flow-down clauses
- Complete required reporting
- Monitor subcontractor performance

## Agenda

### Introduction (5 minutes)
- Welcome and context
- Subcontractor compliance importance
- Overview of requirements

### Compliance Requirements (20 minutes)
- **Flow-Down Clauses**
  - What are flow-down clauses?
  - Common flow-down requirements
  - Applicability to subcontractors
  - Compliance obligations
  
- **Small Business Subcontracting**
  - Subcontracting plans
  - Small business goals
  - Reporting requirements
  - Compliance monitoring

### Subcontractor Management (15 minutes)
- **Selection and Onboarding**
  - Due diligence
  - Agreements and contracts
  - Compliance verification
  - Performance expectations
  
- **Ongoing Management**
  - Performance monitoring
  - Compliance checks
  - Communication
  - Issue resolution

### Reporting Requirements (15 minutes)
- **eSRS (Electronic Subcontracting Reporting System)**
  - ISR (Individual Subcontract Reports)
  - SSR (Summary Subcontract Reports)
  - Submission deadlines
  - Compliance tracking
  
- **Other Reporting**
  - Contract-specific reports
  - Agency requirements
  - Documentation

### Best Practices (3 minutes)
- **Compliance Management**
  - Regular monitoring
  - Documentation
  - Proactive management
  - Issue resolution

## Key Takeaways
1. Subcontractor compliance is prime contractor responsibility
2. Flow-down clauses apply requirements to subcontractors
3. Regular reporting required (ISR/SSR)
4. Proactive management prevents issues
5. Documentation is critical for compliance
