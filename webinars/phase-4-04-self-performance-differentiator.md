# Webinar: Focus on Self Performance Capability as Differentiator

**Phase:** Phase 4 - Business Enhancement (Once)  
**Duration:** 45 minutes  
**Level:** Intermediate  
**Prerequisites:** Past performance experience

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this webinar, participants will be able to:
- Understand self-performance as a competitive advantage
- Document and showcase past performance
- Build self-performance capability
- Position self-performance in proposals
- Leverage past performance for future opportunities

## Agenda

### Introduction (5 minutes)
- Welcome and context
- What is self-performance?
- Why it matters

### Understanding Self-Performance (15 minutes)
- **What is Self-Performance?**
  - Performing work directly
  - Not relying on subcontractors
  - Federal preference
  - Competitive advantage
  
- **Why It's Valued**
  - Control and quality
  - Direct accountability
  - Risk reduction
  - Performance history

### Building Self-Performance (15 minutes)
- **Past Performance Documentation**
  - Project descriptions
  - Results and outcomes
  - CPARS ratings
  - Customer references
  
- **Showcasing Capabilities**
  - Capability statements
  - Proposals
  - Marketing materials
  - Case studies

### Positioning as Differentiator (8 minutes)
- **In Proposals**
  - Highlight self-performance
  - Differentiate from competitors
  - Emphasize advantages
  
- **Marketing**
  - Capability statements
  - Website
  - Presentations

## Key Takeaways
1. Self-performance is a strong differentiator
2. Document past performance thoroughly
3. Showcase self-performance in all materials
4. Build capability through direct performance
5. Position as competitive advantage
