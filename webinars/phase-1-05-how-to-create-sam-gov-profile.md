- Understand annual renewal requirements
- Avoid common registration mistakes

## Presentation Notes

### Opening (5 minutes)
**Welcome and Webinar Overview:**
- "Welcome to today's comprehensive webinar on creating your SAM.gov profile. This is one of the most important steps in Phase 1."
- "SAM.gov registration is mandatory for all federal contractors - you cannot receive federal contracts without it."
- "Today we'll walk through the entire registration process step-by-step, so you're prepared and can complete it successfully."

**What is SAM.gov?**
- System for Award Management
- Primary registration system for federal contractors
- Replaced several legacy systems
- One-stop shop for contractor registration
- Required for all federal contracting

**Why Registration is Mandatory:**
- Cannot receive federal contracts without SAM.gov registration
- Cannot receive payments without SAM.gov registration
- Required before you can bid on opportunities
- Required to receive awards
- Legal requirement

**Overview of Registration Process:**
- Create user account
- Complete entity registration
- Enter business information
- Submit for processing
- Receive UEI (Unique Entity Identifier)
- Registration active (7-10 business days)

### Pre-Registration Preparation (15 minutes)

**Why Preparation Matters:**
- Registration process takes 2-4 hours
- Need all information ready
- Can't save incomplete registration easily
- Preparation makes it smoother
- Avoid frustration and delays

**Information Gathering Checklist:**

**Business Information:**
- Legal business name (exactly as registered with state/IRS)
- DBA (Doing Business As) names, if any
- Business address (physical location)
- Mailing address (if different from physical)
- Business phone number
- Business email address (professional email)
- Business website (if you have one)

**Tax Information:**
- EIN (Employer Identification Number) / TIN
- Business tax classification
- DUNS number (if you have one - may be replaced by UEI)
- NAICS codes (identify 2-5 primary codes before starting)

**Banking Information:**
- Bank name
- Bank account number
- Bank routing number
- Type of account (checking or savings)
- Bank contact information (may be needed)

**Legal & Compliance:**
- Business structure type (LLC, Corporation, Sole Proprietor, Partnership, etc.)
- State of incorporation/registration
- Authorized signatory information (who can sign for business)
- Business registration documents

**Financial Information:**
- Annual receipts (if applicable)
- Number of employees
- Financial statements (may be required for some registrations)
- Revenue information

**Optional Certifications (if applicable):**
- 8(a) Business Development Program eligibility information
- HUBZone location information
- WOSB/EDWOSB certification information
- VOSB/SDVOSB verification information
- Other small business certifications

**Pre-Registration Checklist:**
- ✅ All business information gathered
- ✅ Tax information ready
- ✅ Banking information ready
- ✅ NAICS codes identified
- ✅ 2-4 hours set aside
- ✅ Professional email address ready
- ✅ Documents accessible if needed

### Entity Registration Process (40 minutes)

**Step 1: Start Entity Registration**

**Accessing Registration:**
- Go to SAM.gov
- Click "Get Started" or "Register Entity"
- Create user account first (if you don't have one)
- Login to your account
- Start entity registration

**Creating User Account (if needed):**
- Email address
- Password (strong password required)
- Security questions
- Verify email address
- Account created

**Step 2: Enter Business Information**

**Legal Business Name:**
- Enter exactly as registered with state/IRS
- Must match exactly
- No abbreviations unless that's your legal name
- Check your business registration documents
- Critical - mistakes cause delays

**Business Structure:**
- Select your business type
- LLC, Corporation, Sole Proprietor, Partnership, etc.
- Select from dropdown
- Must match your legal registration

**Business Address:**
- Physical business address
- Mailing address (if different)
- Complete address information
- Verify addresses are correct
- Used for verification

**Contact Information:**
- Primary contact person
- Phone number
- Email address (professional email recommended)
- Website (if applicable)
- All contact information

**Step 3: Core Data**

**EIN/TIN:**
- Enter your Employer Identification Number
- 9-digit number
- Format: XX-XXXXXXX
- Must match IRS records
- Critical information

**NAICS Codes:**
- Select 2-5 primary NAICS codes
- Should match your business activities
- Search by keyword if needed
- Select most relevant codes
- Can add more later if needed

**Business Size Standard:**
- Small business or other than small
- Based on NAICS code and revenue/employees
- System may auto-determine
- Verify accuracy
- Important for set-asides

**Financial Information:**
- Annual receipts (if applicable)
- Number of employees
- Financial data
- May be required for verification
- Provide accurate information

**Banking Information:**
- Bank name
- Account number
- Routing number
- Account type
- Used for payments (EFT)
- Must be accurate

**Step 4: Points of Contact**

**Electronic Business Point of Contact (EBiz POC):**
- Primary point of contact for SAM.gov
- Receives system notifications
- Manages registration
- Can be you or designated person
- Required contact

**Government Business Point of Contact (if applicable):**
- For government communications
- Can be same as EBiz POC
- Optional but recommended
- Business development contact
- Agency outreach contact

**Accounts Receivable Point of Contact:**
- For payment-related communications
- Financial contact
- Payment questions
- Banking matters
- Required contact

**Accounts Payable Point of Contact:**
- For accounts payable matters
- If applicable
- Payment processing
- Financial matters
- Optional contact

**Step 5: Representations & Certifications**

**Review and Complete Reps & Certs:**
- Multiple certifications to complete
- Business type certifications
- Small business certifications (if applicable)
- Socioeconomic certifications (if applicable)
- Compliance certifications

**Answer All Required Questions:**
- Various questions about your business
- Certifications and designations
- Compliance questions
- All questions must be answered
- Complete all sections

**Certify Business Information:**
- Certify information is accurate
- Authorized to certify
- Legal certification
- Important legal step
- Don't certify if unsure

**Accept Terms and Conditions:**
- Review terms
- Accept terms and conditions
- Legal agreement
- Required to proceed
- Read before accepting

**Step 6: Review & Submit**

**Review All Entered Information:**
- Go through each section
- Verify all information
- Check for errors
- Ensure completeness
- Critical step

**Verify Accuracy of All Data:**
- Double-check critical information
- Business name matches exactly
- EIN is correct
- Banking information accurate
- Contact information current

**Submit Registration:**
- Click submit button
- Confirmation screen
- Save confirmation number
- Print or email confirmation
- Registration submitted

**Payment Information:**
- SAM.gov registration is FREE
- No payment required
- No fees for registration
- No payment for renewal
- Completely free service

**Registration Processing:**

**Processing Time:**
- Typically 7-10 business days
- Can be faster or slower
- Depends on information accuracy
- Simple registrations process faster
- Complex may take longer

**UEI Assignment:**
- Unique Entity Identifier assigned
- Replaces DUNS number
- 12-character alphanumeric code
- Received via email
- Save immediately

**Verification Process:**
- System verifies information
- Cross-references with IRS
- Validates business information
- Checks for accuracy
- May require additional information

**Common Issues:**
- Business name mismatch (doesn't match IRS/state)
- EIN validation problems
- Banking information issues
- Incomplete information
- Processing delays

### Post-Registration (10 minutes)

**Immediate Actions:**

**Save UEI:**
- Save your UEI immediately
- Store securely
- Use in all communications
- Add to capability statement
- Critical identifier

**Document Registration Date:**
- Note registration date
- Track expiration date
- Set renewal reminders
- Keep records
- Important for management

**Note Expiration Date:**
- Registrations expire after 1 year
- Must renew annually
- Set reminder 30-60 days before expiration
- Don't let registration expire
- Critical to maintain

**Follow-up Tasks:**

**Review Registration Status:**
- Check status in SAM.gov
- Usually active in 7-10 business days
- Verify all information correct
- Review registration details
- Ensure accuracy

**Verify UEI:**
- Confirm UEI received
- Verify it's correct
- Use in business communications
- Update all materials
- Critical identifier

**Test Login Access:**
- Test your login
- Verify you can access account
- Review registration
- Familiarize yourself with system
- Ensure access works

**Update Marketing Materials:**
- Add UEI to capability statement
- Update business cards if applicable
- Add to email signatures
- Update website if applicable
- Use in all business communications

**Annual Renewal:**

**Renewal Requirement:**
- Must renew annually
- Before expiration date
- Similar process to initial registration
- Update any changed information
- Maintain active registration

**Renewal Reminders:**
- Set calendar reminder
- 30-60 days before expiration
- Don't wait until last minute
- Allow time for processing
- Critical to maintain

**Update Information:**
- Update any changed information during renewal
- Keep information current
- Update within 30 days of changes
- Maintain accuracy
- Legal requirement

### Common Mistakes to Avoid (5 minutes)

**Business Name Mismatch:**
- Must match exactly with IRS/state registration
- Common mistake - causes delays
- Check your business registration documents
- Use exact legal name
- No abbreviations unless that's legal name

**Incomplete Information:**
- Fill out all required fields
- Don't skip sections
- Complete all information
- Incomplete registrations are delayed
- Take time to complete fully

**Wrong Business Structure:**
- Select correct business type
- Must match legal registration
- LLC, Corp, Sole Proprietor, etc.
- Verify your business structure
- Important for compliance

**Banking Information Errors:**
- Verify routing number is correct
- Verify account number
- Contact bank if unsure
- Errors cause payment problems
- Critical for payments

**Not Saving Confirmation:**
- Save confirmation number
- Save UEI immediately
- Print or email confirmation
- Keep records
- Important for reference

### Troubleshooting (5 minutes)

**Entity Validation Problems:**
- Business name doesn't match IRS
- Solution: Ensure name matches exactly with IRS records
- Check your EIN confirmation letter
- Verify with state registration
- May need to contact IRS or state

**UEI Not Received:**
- Check email spam folder
- May take 24-48 hours
- Contact SAM.gov help desk if not received
- Verify email address is correct
- Check registration status

**Cannot Submit Registration:**
- Ensure all required fields completed
- Check for error messages
- Review each section
- Complete all required information
- Contact help desk if needed

**Banking Information Not Accepted:**
- Verify routing number is correct
- Verify account number
- Contact bank to verify
- Check account is active
- Use correct account type

**Help Resources:**
- SAM.gov Help Desk: 1-866-606-8220
- SAM.gov User Guide (online)
- Federal Service Desk (online)
- Email support available
- Get help when needed

**Q&A:**
- "What if I don't have an EIN?" - You need an EIN to register. Apply with IRS first.
- "Can I register as a sole proprietor?" - Yes, but consider business structure first.
- "How long does registration last?" - 1 year, must renew annually.
- "Is registration really free?" - Yes, completely free, no fees.
- "What happens if my registration expires?" - You cannot receive contracts or payments until renewed.
