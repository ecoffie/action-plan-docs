# Webinar: Identify Mid Size Mentor

**Phase:** Phase 4 - Business Enhancement (Once)  
**Duration:** 45 minutes  
**Level:** Intermediate  
**Prerequisites:** Understanding of mentor relationships

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this webinar, participants will be able to:
- Understand benefits of mid-size mentors
- Identify potential mid-size mentor companies
- Approach mid-size companies
- Structure mentor relationships
- Maximize mentor benefits

## Agenda

### Introduction (5 minutes)
- Welcome and context
- Mid-size mentors vs. large primes
- Benefits overview

### Benefits of Mid-Size Mentors (15 minutes)
- **Advantages**
  - More accessible than large primes
  - Similar growth journey experience
  - More personalized attention
  - Joint venture opportunities
  - Strategic partnerships
  
- **What Mid-Size Mentors Offer**
  - Business development support
  - Knowledge transfer
  - Access to larger opportunities
  - Industry expertise

### Finding Mid-Size Mentors (15 minutes)
- **Identifying Candidates**
  - Size criteria (mid-size range)
  - Industry alignment
  - Complementary capabilities
  - Growth trajectory
  
- **Research Methods**
  - Industry databases
  - Networking events
  - Industry associations
  - Referrals

### Approaching Mid-Size Companies (8 minutes)
- **Outreach Strategy**
  - Value proposition
  - Mutual benefits
  - Professional approach
  - Relationship building

### Relationship Structure (2 minutes)
- **Mentor Arrangements**
  - Formal vs. informal
  - Mutual benefits
  - Long-term focus

## Key Takeaways
1. Mid-size mentors offer unique advantages
2. More accessible than large primes
3. Similar growth experience creates connection
4. Strategic relationships benefit both parties
5. Mid-size mentors facilitate growth
