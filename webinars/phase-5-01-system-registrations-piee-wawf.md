# Webinar: System Registrations (PIEE, WAWF)

**Phase:** Phase 5 - Contract Management  
**Duration:** 60 minutes  
**Level:** Intermediate  
**Prerequisites:** Contract award received or imminent

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this webinar, participants will be able to:
- Understand PIEE and WAWF systems
- Register for PIEE access
- Register for WAWF access
- Navigate system functions
- Use systems for contract management tasks

## Agenda

### Introduction (5 minutes)
- Welcome and Phase 5 context
- Required systems overview
- Why registration is critical

### PIEE Registration (20 minutes)
- **What is PIEE?**
  - Procurement Integrated Enterprise Environment
  - DOD contract management system
  - Functions and capabilities
  
- **Registration Process**
  - Accessing PIEE
  - Account creation
  - Entity registration
  - Access levels
  - Approval process
  
- **PIEE Functions**
  - Contract award acceptance
  - Invoice submission
  - Contract modifications
  - Performance reporting
  - Document access

### WAWF Registration (20 minutes)
- **What is WAWF?**
  - Wide Area Workflow
  - Receiving and invoicing system
  - Payment processing
  
- **Registration Process**
  - Accessing WAWF
  - Account setup
  - Entity registration
  - EFT setup
  - Training requirements
  
- **WAWF Functions**
  - Receiving reports
  - Invoice creation and submission
  - Payment status tracking
  - Document management
  - Quality assurance

### System Navigation (10 minutes)
- **Getting Started**
  - Login procedures
  - Dashboard navigation
  - Help resources
  - Training materials
  
- **Common Tasks**
  - Submitting invoices
  - Creating receiving reports
  - Tracking status
  - Accessing documents

### Best Practices (3 minutes)
- **System Usage**
  - Regular access
  - Training completion
  - Document management
  - Support resources

## Key Takeaways
1. PIEE and WAWF registration required for contract management
2. Register early - before you need to use systems
3. Complete training for effective system use
4. Systems streamline contract management processes
5. Keep access credentials secure and accessible
