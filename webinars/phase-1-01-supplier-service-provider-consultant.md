# Webinar: Supplier v Service Provider v Consultant

**Phase:** Phase 1 - Setup (Once)  
**Duration:** 45-60 minutes  
**Level:** Beginner  
**Prerequisites:** None

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this webinar, participants will be able to:
- Understand the differences between Supplier, Service Provider, and Consultant business models
- Determine which structure best fits their business
- Understand the implications of each structure for federal contracting
- Make an informed decision about their business positioning

### Understanding the Three Business Models (20 minutes)

#### Supplier
- **Definition**: Businesses that provide products/goods
- **Characteristics**:
  - Deliver tangible products
  - Manufacturing or distribution focus
  - Inventory management
  - Product specifications and compliance
- **Federal Contracting Considerations**:
  - Product-based solicitations
  - GSA Schedule (products)
  - Specifications compliance
  - Delivery requirements
- **Examples**: Equipment manufacturers, software distributors, supplies vendors

#### Service Provider
- **Definition**: Businesses that deliver services
- **Characteristics**:
  - Deliver services/intangible deliverables
  - Labor-based contracts
  - Performance-based outcomes
  - Ongoing service delivery
- **Federal Contracting Considerations**:
  - Labor categories and rates
  - Service-level agreements
  - Performance metrics
  - Time & materials or fixed-price services
- **Examples**: IT services, consulting firms, maintenance services, professional services

#### Consultant
- **Definition**: Businesses providing advisory/expert services
- **Characteristics**:
  - Expert advice and recommendations
  - Analysis and strategic guidance
  - Temporary or project-based engagement
  - Deliverable-based (reports, plans, analysis)
- **Federal Contracting Considerations**:
  - Statement of Work (SOW) focus
  - Expertise and qualifications critical
  - Deliverable-based pricing
  - Key personnel requirements
- **Examples**: Strategic consultants, advisory services, technical experts

### Comparing the Models (15 minutes)
- **Pricing Structures**
  - Supplier: Unit pricing, volume discounts
  - Service Provider: Hourly rates, fixed-price services
  - Consultant: Project-based, deliverable-based
  
- **Contract Types**
  - Supplier: Firm-fixed-price, delivery orders
  - Service Provider: Time & materials, fixed-price labor hours
  - Consultant: Fixed-price, cost-plus-fixed-fee
  
- **Competition Factors**
  - Supplier: Price, quality, delivery time
  - Service Provider: Technical approach, past performance, team
  - Consultant: Expertise, qualifications, approach

  - NAICS code selection impact
