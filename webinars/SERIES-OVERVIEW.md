# Federal Contracting Roadmap Webinar Series - Overview

## Series Description
A comprehensive webinar series that breaks down the federal contracting process into actionable steps, based on the Federal Contracting Roadmap. Each webinar builds upon the previous one, providing attendees with a complete understanding of how to successfully enter and compete in the federal marketplace.

## Target Audience
- Small businesses new to federal contracting
- Businesses looking to expand into federal markets
- Existing contractors seeking to improve their win rates
- Consultants and advisors working with federal contractors

## Series Learning Objectives
By the end of this series, participants will:
1. Complete all Phase 1 setup requirements (SAM.gov, NAICS, capability statements)
2. Understand how to find and respond to federal contracting opportunities (Phase 2)
3. Develop skills for ongoing business development and relationship building (Phase 3)
4. Know how to enhance their competitive position through certifications and partnerships (Phase 4)
5. Manage contracts effectively using required systems and compliance processes (Phase 5)

## Complete Webinar Series

Based on the Federal Contracting Roadmap's 5-Phase Structure, with individual webinars for each topic:

### Phase 1: Setup (Once) - 8 Webinars

1. **Supplier v Service Provider v Consultant** (45-60 min)
   - Understanding business model differences and federal contracting implications

2. **DUNS and UEI** (30-45 min)
   - Understanding entity identifiers and the transition from DUNS to UEI

3. **Creating Professional Email** (30-45 min)
   - Setting up professional email domains and business communications

4. **How to Identify NAICS** (45-60 min)
   - Identifying and selecting appropriate NAICS codes for your business

5. **How to Create a SAM.gov Profile** (90 min)
   - Complete step-by-step guide to SAM.gov registration

6. **DSBS** (30-45 min)
   - Dynamic Small Business Search registration and optimization

7. **Benefits / What to say to Apex Accelerator** (45-60 min)
   - Understanding Apex Accelerator services and how to engage

8. **What to put on Capability Statement / Differentiators** (60-75 min)
   - Creating effective capability statements and identifying differentiators

### Phase 2: Bidding (Repeat) - 5 Webinars

9. **How to Find Bid Opportunities** (60 min)
   - Using multiple sources to find federal contracting opportunities

10. **Assemble Team Based on Opportunities** (60 min)
    - Team formation strategies and partner selection

11. **Apply for Vendor/Supplier Credit** (45-60 min)
    - Building credit and securing vendor/supplier credit and bonding

12. **Respond to Opportunity (RFP, RFQ, RFI, Task Orders)** (90 min)
    - Complete proposal development and submission process

13. **Evaluate Bid Results** (30-45 min)
    - Understanding results, debriefings, and continuous improvement

### Phase 3: Business Development (Repeat) - 6 Webinars

14. **Identify Top 25 Buyers & Future Bids (NOT ON SAM)** (60 min)
    - Researching agencies and finding opportunities before they're posted

15. **Setup and Attend Meetings with Government Buyers** (60 min)
    - Building relationships with contracting officers and key decision makers

16. **Attend Industry Events** (45 min)
    - Maximizing value from industry days, trade shows, and conferences

17. **Attend Site Visits** (30-45 min)
    - Preparing for and conducting effective site visits

18. **Get on Supplier List for Top 25 Federal Suppliers** (45-60 min)
    - Registering with prime contractors and supplier diversity programs

19. **Monitor Contract Awards and Identify Sub Opportunities** (45-60 min)
    - Tracking awards, IDIQs, and subcontracting opportunities

### Phase 4: Business Enhancement (Once) - 7 Webinars

20. **Apply for Small Business Certification** (60 min)
    - Understanding and applying for HUBZone, WOSB, VOSB certifications

21. **8(a) Certification** (90 min)
    - Complete guide to the 8(a) Business Development Program

22. **Mentor Protégé Program** (60 min)
    - Finding mentors and structuring mentor-protégé relationships

23. **Focus on Self Performance Capability as Differentiator** (45 min)
    - Building and showcasing self-performance as competitive advantage

24. **Find Better Partners** (45 min)
    - Evaluating and selecting strategic partners

25. **Identify Mid Size Mentor** (45 min)
    - Finding and working with mid-size mentor companies

26. **Speak at an Event** (30-45 min)
    - Building thought leadership through speaking opportunities

### Phase 5: Contract Management - 4 Webinars

27. **System Registrations (PIEE, WAWF)** (60 min)
    - Registering for and using required contract management systems

28. **Subcontractor Compliance** (60 min)
    - Managing subcontractor relationships and compliance requirements

29. **Project Compliance** (60 min)
    - Meeting contract terms, performance requirements, and quality standards

30. **Communication** (45 min)
    - Effective communication with contracting officers and stakeholders

---

**Total: 30 Individual Webinars**
- Phase 1: 8 webinars (foundation setup - complete once)
- Phase 2: 5 webinars (bidding process - repeat as needed)
- Phase 3: 6 webinars (business development - repeat ongoing)
- Phase 4: 7 webinars (business enhancement - complete strategically)
- Phase 5: 4 webinars (contract management - ongoing)

## Recommended Learning Path

1. **Complete Phase 1** (all 8 webinars) before moving forward
2. **Cycle through Phase 2 and Phase 3** as repeatable processes
3. **Pursue Phase 4** strategically based on business needs
4. **Apply Phase 5** when contracts are awarded
