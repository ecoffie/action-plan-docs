**Phase:** Phase 1 - Setup (Once)  
**Duration:** 30-45 minutes  
**Level:** Beginner

- Understand what DUNS and UEI are
- Know how DUNS relates to UEI
- Understand the transition from DUNS to UEI
- Know how to obtain/get assigned these identifiers
- Understand their importance in federal contracting

**Data Universal Numbering System:**
- 9-digit unique identifier
- Created by Dun & Bradstreet
- Long-standing standard
- Used for business identification
- Credit and business verification
- Historical importance

**Historical Importance:**
- Long-standing requirement for federal contracting
- Used for entity identification for many years
- Required for SAM.gov registration (historically)
- Used for credit and business verification
- Business identification standard
- Legacy system

**How to Obtain DUNS:**

**DUNS Registration Process:**
- Register through Dun & Bradstreet website
- Free for government contracting purposes
- Online registration available
- Required business information
- Verification process
- Timeline for receipt (usually 1-2 business days)

**Free for Government Contracting:**
- DUNS is free if you indicate government contracting purpose
- No cost for federal contractors
- Important to specify government contracting use
- Free registration available
- No fees required

**Dun & Bradstreet Website:**
- dnb.com/duns-number
- Register online
- Complete registration form
- Provide business information
- Verification process
- Receive DUNS number

**Required Information:**
- Business name (legal name)
- Business address
- Phone number
- Email address
- Business structure type
- Authorized signatory information

**Timeline for Receipt:**
- Usually received within 1-2 business days
- May take longer in some cases
- Email notification when ready
- Can check status online
- Contact support if delayed
