# Webinar: How to Identify NAICS

**Phase:** Phase 1 - Setup (Once)  
**Duration:** 45-60 minutes  
**Level:** Beginner  
**Prerequisites:** None

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this webinar, participants will be able to:
- Understand what NAICS codes are and why they matter
- Use tools and resources to identify appropriate NAICS codes
- Select primary and secondary NAICS codes for their business
- Understand how NAICS codes affect federal contracting opportunities
- Avoid common NAICS code selection mistakes
