# Webinar: Attend Site Visits

**Phase:** Phase 3 - Business Development (Repeat)  
**Duration:** 30-45 minutes  
**Level:** Intermediate  
**Prerequisites:** Understanding of business development

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this webinar, participants will be able to:
- Understand what site visits are
- Prepare for site visits
- Maximize value from site visits
- Demonstrate capabilities effectively
- Follow up after visits

## Agenda

### Introduction (5 minutes)
- Welcome and context
- What are site visits?
- Why they matter

### Understanding Site Visits (10 minutes)
- **Types of Site Visits**
  - Facility tours
  - Capability demonstrations
  - Pre-award site visits
  - Customer site visits
  
- **Purpose**
  - Demonstrate capabilities
  - Build relationships
  - Understand customer needs
  - Gather intelligence

### Preparation (10 minutes)
- **Before the Visit**
  - Understand purpose
  - Prepare questions
  - Organize facility/demo
  - Prepare materials
  - Professional presentation
  
- **What to Prepare**
  - Facility readiness
  - Demonstration materials
  - Capability presentations
  - Key personnel availability

### During the Visit (8 minutes)
- **Best Practices**
  - Professional presentation
  - Demonstrate capabilities
  - Listen to needs
  - Ask good questions
  - Build rapport

### Follow-up (5 minutes)
- **After the Visit**
  - Thank you communication
  - Address questions
  - Provide additional information
  - Maintain contact

## Key Takeaways
1. Site visits demonstrate real capabilities
2. Preparation shows professionalism
3. Active listening reveals customer needs
4. Follow-up reinforces commitment
5. Site visits build strong relationships
