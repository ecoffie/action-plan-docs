# Webinar: Project Compliance

**Phase:** Phase 5 - Contract Management  
**Duration:** 60 minutes  
**Level:** Intermediate  
**Prerequisites:** Active contract

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this webinar, participants will be able to:
- Understand contract terms and conditions
- Meet performance requirements
- Maintain quality standards
- Manage deliverables
- Ensure ongoing compliance

## Agenda

### Introduction (5 minutes)
- Welcome and context
- Project compliance importance
- Contract terms overview

### Understanding Contract Terms (15 minutes)
- **Key Clauses**
  - Statement of Work (SOW)
  - Performance requirements
  - Quality standards
  - Deliverable specifications
  - Timeline and milestones
  
- **Compliance Obligations**
  - What's required
  - Performance metrics
  - Reporting requirements
  - Documentation needs

### Performance Management (20 minutes)
- **Meeting Requirements**
  - Deliverable management
  - Quality assurance
  - Timeline compliance
  - Performance metrics
  
- **Quality Standards**
  - Quality management
  - Inspection and testing
  - Defect management
  - Continuous improvement
  
- **CPARS (Contractor Performance Assessment)**
  - Understanding CPARS
  - Performance ratings
  - Impact on future opportunities
  - Managing ratings

### Deliverable Management (12 minutes)
- **Deliverable Requirements**
  - Understanding specifications
  - Format requirements
  - Submission procedures
  - Acceptance process
  
- **Managing Deliverables**
  - Tracking and scheduling
  - Quality review
  - Timely submission
  - Acceptance management

### Ongoing Compliance (6 minutes)
- **Monitoring and Maintenance**
  - Regular compliance checks
  - Performance monitoring
  - Issue identification
  - Corrective actions

## Key Takeaways
1. Understanding contract terms is fundamental to compliance
2. Performance quality impacts CPARS and future opportunities
3. Deliverable management requires attention to detail
4. Ongoing monitoring prevents compliance issues
5. Quality standards must be maintained throughout
