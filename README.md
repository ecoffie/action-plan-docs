# Federal Contracting Roadmap Webinar Series

## Overview
This repository contains the complete structure, content, and materials for a comprehensive webinar series based on the Federal Contracting Roadmap. The series is designed to guide businesses through the process of successfully entering and navigating the federal contracting marketplace.

## Structure
- **`roadmap/`** - Contains the federal contracting roadmap document and related materials
- **`webinars/`** - Individual webinar outlines, scripts, and resources
- **`website/`** - Webinar landing page and registration materials
- **`assets/`** - Images, slides, handouts, and other supporting materials

## Getting Started
1. Review the roadmap in `roadmap/federal-contracting-roadmap.md`
2. Check the webinar series overview in `webinars/SERIES-OVERVIEW.md`
3. Explore individual webinar outlines in `webinars/`
