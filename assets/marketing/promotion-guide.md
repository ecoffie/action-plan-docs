# Webinar Series Promotion Guide

## Marketing Strategy Overview

### Target Audience
- Small businesses new to federal contracting
- Existing businesses looking to expand into federal markets
- Business consultants and advisors
- Chambers of commerce members
- Economic development organizations

### Key Messages
1. **Clear Path**: Learn the complete federal contracting process
2. **Expert Guidance**: Based on proven federal contracting roadmap
3. **Practical Tools**: Get actionable templates and resources
4. **Community**: Join others on the same journey
5. **Success**: Build the foundation for federal contract wins

## Pre-Launch (4-6 weeks before)

### Content Creation
- [ ] Create series landing page
- [ ] Develop email marketing templates
- [ ] Write social media posts (LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook)
- [ ] Create video teaser/promotional video
- [ ] Design graphics and banners
- [ ] Prepare blog posts/articles

### Email Campaigns
- [ ] Announcement email to existing list
- [ ] Series introduction email
- [ ] Individual webinar promotion emails
- [ ] Reminder emails for registrations
- [ ] Follow-up emails after each webinar

### Social Media
- [ ] LinkedIn posts (weekly leading up to launch)
- [ ] Twitter/X announcements
- [ ] Facebook posts and events
- [ ] Instagram graphics (if relevant)
- [ ] LinkedIn articles about federal contracting

### Partnerships
- [ ] Reach out to Small Business Development Centers (SBDCs)
- [ ] Contact local chambers of commerce
- [ ] Partner with economic development organizations
- [ ] Connect with business associations
- [ ] Reach out to veteran business organizations

## Launch (1-2 weeks before first webinar)

### Promotion Activities
- [ ] Open registration
- [ ] Launch landing page
- [ ] Send registration announcement emails
- [ ] Post on all social media channels
- [ ] Reach out to partner organizations for promotion
- [ ] Consider paid advertising (LinkedIn, Google Ads)

### Registration Incentives (if applicable)
- [ ] Early bird pricing/discounts
- [ ] Bundle pricing for full series
- [ ] Free resources for early registrants
- [ ] Bonus materials

## During Series (Ongoing)

### Before Each Webinar
- [ ] Send reminder email 3 days before
- [ ] Send reminder email 1 day before
- [ ] Send reminder email 1 hour before (with link)
- [ ] Post social media reminders
- [ ] Send calendar invites

### During Each Webinar
- [ ] Live social media posts
- [ ] Encourage sharing
- [ ] Collect questions for follow-up
- [ ] Promote next webinar in series

### After Each Webinar
- [ ] Send thank you email with recording link
- [ ] Share resources and handouts
- [ ] Request feedback/survey
- [ ] Promote next webinar
- [ ] Post webinar highlights on social media

## Post-Series

### Follow-up
- [ ] Send complete series recap
- [ ] Provide access to all recordings
- [ ] Share resource library
- [ ] Request testimonials
- [ ] Survey for feedback and improvements

### Repurposing Content
- [ ] Create on-demand course version
- [ ] Develop blog posts from webinar content
- [ ] Create downloadable guides
- [ ] Package as premium resource
- [ ] Use for future marketing
