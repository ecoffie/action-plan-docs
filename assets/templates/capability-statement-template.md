# Capability Statement Template

## Overview
A capability statement is your business's resume for federal contracting. It should be professional, concise, and highlight your unique value proposition.

## Format Options
- **One-page version**: For initial meetings and quick reference
- **Multi-page version**: For detailed presentations and formal submissions

## Design Guidelines

### Visual Elements
- **Professional Layout**: Clean, organized, easy to scan
- **Company Logo**: Prominently displayed
- **Color Scheme**: Professional colors, aligned with brand
- **Fonts**: Easy-to-read, professional fonts
- **White Space**: Don't overcrowd - use white space effectively

### Content Guidelines
- **Keep It Current**: Update regularly with new contracts and achievements
- **Be Specific**: Use numbers, metrics, and concrete examples
- **Focus on Benefits**: Highlight outcomes, not just services
- **Tailor When Possible**: Adjust content for specific agencies or opportunities

### Key Principles
1. **Clarity**: Easy to understand what you do
2. **Credibility**: Evidence of past success
3. **Differentiation**: What makes you unique
4. **Relevance**: Aligned with federal needs
5. **Professionalism**: High-quality presentation

---

## One-Page Version Tips
- Use columns to maximize space
- Prioritize most important information
- Use bullet points instead of paragraphs
- Keep font size readable (minimum 10pt)
- Include only top 2-3 past performance items
- Focus on strongest differentiators

## Multi-Page Version Tips
- Page 1: Overview and core competencies
- Page 2: Detailed past performance
- Page 3: Certifications, compliance, team
- Additional pages: Case studies, detailed project descriptions

---

## Before You Finalize

### Review Checklist
- [ ] All contact information is current
- [ ] UEI and registration numbers are correct
- [ ] NAICS codes match your SAM.gov registration
- [ ] Past performance examples are relevant and recent
- [ ] Differentiators are clear and compelling
- [ ] No typos or grammatical errors
- [ ] Design is professional and consistent
- [ ] Information is accurate and verifiable

### Version Control
- Save dated versions (e.g., "Capability Statement - 2024-01")
- Track updates and changes
- Maintain archive of previous versions
