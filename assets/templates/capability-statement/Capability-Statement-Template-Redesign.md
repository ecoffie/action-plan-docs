- **Contact Info**: 10pt, Medium Gray
- **UEI/CAGE**: 10pt, Bold, Medium Gray

## Design Elements

### Header Section
- **Background**: White or subtle gradient
- **Border Bottom**: 4px solid Primary Blue
- **Logo Placement**: Top left, max height 1.5"
- **Company Name**: Prominent, below logo
- **Contact Block**: Right-aligned or below name
- **Visual Separator**: Thin accent line (2px) in Medium Blue

### Section Design
- **Section Headers**: 
  - Background: Primary Blue (#1e3c72)
  - Text: White, 14pt, Bold, Uppercase
  - Padding: 8pt top/bottom, 12pt left/right
  - Border radius: 4px top corners
  
- **Content Areas**:
  - Background: White
  - Padding: 12pt
  - Spacing: 8pt between items
  
- **Alternating Sections** (optional):
  - Light Gray background (#f5f5f5)
  - White background

### Past Performance Cards
- **Card Style**: Subtle border, light shadow
- **Background**: White
- **Border**: 1px solid #e0e0e0
- **Padding**: 12pt
- **Spacing**: 12pt between cards

### Differentiators
- **Icon**: Checkmark (✓) or arrow (→) in Primary Blue
- **Format**: Bullet points with bold text
- **Spacing**: 10pt between items

### Footer
- **Background**: Light Gray (#f5f5f5)
- **Text**: 9pt, Medium Gray
- **Content**: Certifications, additional info
- **Height**: Auto-fit content

## Visual Enhancements

### Color Accents
- Use Primary Blue for:
  - Section headers
  - Important numbers (contract values)
  - UEI/CAGE codes
  - Differentiator icons
  
- Use Medium Blue for:
  - Subtle accents
  - Secondary information
  - Section dividers

### Typography Hierarchy
1. **Company Name** - Largest, most prominent
2. **Section Headers** - Clear, bold
3. **Project Titles** - Bold, readable
4. **Body Text** - Clear, scannable
5. **Contact Info** - Small but readable

### White Space
- Generous margins (0.75")
- 12pt spacing between sections
- 8pt spacing between items
- Avoid clutter - less is more

### Icons & Graphics
- Minimal use of icons
- Professional style
- Consistent sizing (16-20px)
- Primary Blue color
