# Federal Contracting Resource Directory

## Official Government Websites

### Registration & Compliance
- **SAM.gov** (System for Award Management)
  - URL: https://sam.gov
  - Purpose: Primary registration system for federal contractors
  - Key Features: Entity registration, opportunity search, past performance

- **SAM.gov Contract Opportunities**
  - URL: https://sam.gov
  - Purpose: Search and respond to federal contracting opportunities (consolidated into SAM.gov)
  - Key Features: Opportunity search, document downloads, submission

- **Small Business Administration (SBA)**
  - URL: https://www.sba.gov
  - Purpose: Small business resources and certifications
  - Key Features: 8(a), HUBZone, WOSB programs, counseling

### Market Research
- **USAspending.gov**
  - URL: https://www.usaspending.gov
  - Purpose: Federal spending data and transparency
  - Key Features: Agency spending, contractor awards, historical data

- **Federal Procurement Data System (FPDS)**
  - URL: https://www.fpds.gov
  - Purpose: Comprehensive federal procurement data
  - Key Features: Award data, contract history, agency reports

- **GSA eBuy**
  - URL: https://www.ebuy.gsa.gov
  - Purpose: GSA Schedule opportunity search
  - Key Features: RFQ/RFP search, submission platform

### Agency-Specific
- **GSA (General Services Administration)**
  - URL: https://www.gsa.gov
  - Purpose: GSA Schedule program information
  - Key Features: Schedule contracts, eBuy, training

- **DOD SBIR/STTR**
  - URL: https://www.defensesbirsttr.mil
  - Purpose: Small Business Innovation Research
  - Key Features: Technology R&D opportunities

- **NASA SBIR/STTR**
  - URL: https://sbir.nasa.gov
  - Purpose: NASA innovation programs
  - Key Features: Technology opportunities

## Certifications & Set-Asides

### Small Business Programs
- **8(a) Business Development**
  - SBA program for socially/economically disadvantaged
  - 9-year program with business development support

- **HUBZone Program**
  - Historically Underutilized Business Zone
  - Must be located in HUBZone and employ HUBZone residents

- **WOSB/EDWOSB**
  - Women-Owned Small Business
  - Economically Disadvantaged Women-Owned Small Business

- **VOSB/SDVOSB**
  - Veteran-Owned Small Business
  - Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business

### Certification Resources

- **VA Vets First Verification**
