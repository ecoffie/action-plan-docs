# SAM.gov Registration Checklist

Use this checklist to ensure you have everything ready before starting your SAM.gov registration.

## Pre-Registration Preparation

### Business Information
- [ ] Legal business name (exactly as registered)
- [ ] DBA (Doing Business As) names, if applicable
- [ ] Business address (physical location)
- [ ] Mailing address (if different)
- [ ] Business phone number
- [ ] Business email address
- [ ] Business website (if applicable)

### Tax Information
- [ ] EIN (Employer Identification Number) / TIN
- [ ] Business tax classification
- [ ] DUNS number (may be replaced by UEI)
- [ ] NAICS codes (identify 2-5 primary codes)

### Banking Information
- [ ] Bank name
- [ ] Bank account number
- [ ] Bank routing number
- [ ] Type of account (checking/savings)

### Legal & Compliance
- [ ] Business structure type (LLC, Corp, Sole Proprietor, etc.)
- [ ] State of incorporation/registration
- [ ] Authorized signatory information
- [ ] Business registration documents

### Financial Information
- [ ] Annual receipts (if applicable)
- [ ] Number of employees
- [ ] Financial statements (may be required for some registrations)

### Optional Certifications (if applicable)
- [ ] 8(a) Business Development Program eligibility
- [ ] HUBZone location information
- [ ] WOSB/EDWOSB certification
- [ ] VOSB/SDVOSB verification
- [ ] Small Business Administration (SBA) certifications

## Registration Process Steps

### Step 1: Create Account
- [ ] Go to SAM.gov
- [ ] Create user account
- [ ] Verify email address
- [ ] Set up security questions

### Step 2: Entity Registration
- [ ] Enter business legal name
- [ ] Enter business structure
- [ ] Enter business address
- [ ] Enter contact information
- [ ] Upload required documents (if any)

### Step 3: Core Data
- [ ] Enter EIN/TIN
- [ ] Enter NAICS codes
- [ ] Select business size standard
- [ ] Enter financial information
- [ ] Enter banking information

### Step 4: Points of Contact
- [ ] Designate Electronic Business Point of Contact (EBiz POC)
- [ ] Designate Government Business Point of Contact (if applicable)
- [ ] Designate Accounts Receivable Point of Contact
- [ ] Designate Accounts Payable Point of Contact
- [ ] Enter alternate contacts (optional)

### Step 5: Representations & Certifications
- [ ] Review and complete Reps & Certs
- [ ] Answer all required questions
- [ ] Certify business information
- [ ] Accept terms and conditions

### Step 6: Review & Submit
- [ ] Review all entered information
- [ ] Verify accuracy of all data
- [ ] Submit registration
- [ ] Save confirmation number
- [ ] Print/email confirmation

## Post-Registration

### Immediate Actions
- [ ] Save UEI (Unique Entity Identifier)
- [ ] Document registration date
- [ ] Note expiration date (1 year from registration)
- [ ] Set calendar reminder for renewal (30-60 days before expiration)

### Follow-up Tasks
- [ ] Review registration status (usually takes 7-10 business days)
- [ ] Verify UEI appears correctly
- [ ] Test login access
- [ ] Update any marketing materials with UEI
- [ ] Share registration confirmation with team

## Important Reminders

- **Annual Renewal Required**: SAM.gov registrations expire after 1 year
- **Keep Information Updated**: Update registration within 30 days of any changes
- **Save All Documents**: Keep copies of all submitted documents
- **Monitor Email**: Check email regularly for SAM.gov communications
- **Security**: Keep login credentials secure and don't share accounts

## Common Issues & Solutions

### Issue: Entity validation problems
**Solution**: Ensure business name matches exactly with IRS/state registration

### Issue: UEI not received
**Solution**: Check email spam folder, may take 24-48 hours

### Issue: Cannot submit registration
**Solution**: Ensure all required fields are completed, check for error messages

### Issue: Banking information not accepted
**Solution**: Verify routing number is correct, contact bank if needed
