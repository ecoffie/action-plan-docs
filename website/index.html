Phase 1: Setup (Once)

⏱️ 90 minutes 📊 Beginner 🔄 Once

Establish your foundation: choose business structure, understand DUNS/UEI, identify NAICS codes, create SAM.gov profile, connect with Apex Accelerator, and develop your capability statement. Complete this phase thoroughly before moving forward.

Learn More

Phase 2: Bidding (Repeat)

⏱️ 90 minutes 📊 Beginner/Intermediate 🔄 Repeat

Find and respond to opportunities: review immediate bid opportunities, assemble teams, apply for vendor/supplier credit, respond to RFPs/RFQs/RFIs/Task Orders, and evaluate bid results. This is an ongoing, repeatable process.

Learn More

Phase 3: Business Development (Repeat)

⏱️ 90 minutes 📊 Intermediate 🔄 Repeat

Build relationships and identify opportunities: find top 25 buyers and future bids (not on SAM), meet with government buyers, attend industry events and site visits, get on supplier lists, and monitor contract awards for subcontracting opportunities.

Learn More

Phase 4: Business Enhancement (Once)

⏱️ 60 minutes 📊 Intermediate 🔄 Once

Strengthen your competitive position: apply for small business certifications, navigate 8(a) program, understand mentor-protégé programs, leverage self-performance as differentiator, find strategic partners and mentors, and build thought leadership.

Learn More

Phase 5: Contract Management

⏱️ 60 minutes 📊 Intermediate 🔄 Ongoing

Execute successfully: register for required systems (PIEE, WAWF), ensure subcontractor compliance, maintain project compliance, and establish effective communication with contracting officers and stakeholders for contract success.
