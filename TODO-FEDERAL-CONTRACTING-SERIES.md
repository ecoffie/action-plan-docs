## QUICK REFERENCE: What "Complete" Looks Like

A complete webinar file should have:
- **Header**: Phase, Duration, Level, Prerequisites
- **Learning Objectives**: 4-5 bullet points
- **Agenda**: Detailed breakdown with timing
- **Key Takeaways**: 4-5 bullet points
- **Resources to Share**: List of materials
- **Follow-up Materials**: Additional resources
- **Presentation Notes**: DETAILED talking points for each agenda section (this is what's missing in incomplete webinars)
- **Course Structure Reference**: Connection to GovCon Giants/Dummies Course content
- **Slide Outline**: 15-22 slide topics

**Target Length**: 300-600+ lines per webinar
