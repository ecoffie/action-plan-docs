# Dummies Course (FREE Course) to Phase 1 Webinar Mapping

## Course Structure

The Dummies Course is a FREE beginner course covering Phase 1 setup topics.

## Course Outline (18 Days)

From "Outline Govcon for Dummies.docx":

**7-Step Formula:**
1. Create Corp - 30 min
2. Request EIN - 30 min  
3. Open Bank Account
4. Getting DUNS - 45 min - 1 hour (fill out form)
5. Choosing NAICS - 30 min - 1 hour (mostly research)
6. SAM registration - 3 hours (going through documents)
7. After SAM is approved - go back and do DSBS

**Course Days:**
- Day 1: What is a government contract?
- Day 2-9: Basic questions and concepts
- Day 10: How do I start? 5 Step Formula
- Day 11: Create Legal Entity
- Day 12: EIN
- Day 13: Choose NAICS / DUNS
- Day 14: Choose NAICS (continued)
- Day 15: SAM registration
- Day 16: How do I start getting paid?
- Day 17: Is this the only course I need?
- Day 18: Big Thank You and Surprise
